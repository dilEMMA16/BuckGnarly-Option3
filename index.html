About
Phil and Pat first met back in high school, and openly admit their long-term bromance. Fueled by a pantheon of craft beers, "old" man muscles, and their lovely muses, Julia and and Angie, the two musicians have paired up once again to deliver high-caliber music to the people via Buck Gnarly.

Phil, an Aquarius, enjoys long walks absurdly late at night and has an affinity for greenscreening. He can often be found wearing cargo pants and grilling despite any weather Minnesota throws at him.



Pat is happiest watching videos from the dark web (that is, if the Minnesota Wild aren't playing). He loves Sinatra and spending weekends at his lake-side cabin.
